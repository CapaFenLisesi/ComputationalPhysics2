Definitions and notations

Combining Eqs. (12) and (15) we obtain the energy functional $$ \begin{equation} E[\Phi] = \sum_{\mu=1}^N \langle \mu | \hat{h}_0 | \mu \rangle + \frac{1}{2}\sum_{{\mu}=1}^N\sum_{{\nu}=1}^N \langle \mu\nu|\hat{v}|\mu\nu\rangle_{\mathrm{AS}}. \tag{16} \end{equation} $$ which we will use as our starting point for the Hartree-Fock calculations later in this course.
